About Us

We partner our with clients to provide technical expertise and integrated infrastructure to optimize the value, growth, and sustainability of their global supply chain. Our nationwide solutions for electronic asset management and network of facilities, skilled engineers and a high level of standardization, supports rapid implementation and economies of scale to allow us to grow with our customers.

The nationwide supply chain networks of Hi-Tech and consumer electronics OEMs(Original Equipment Manufacturer) around the nation are massive in both scope and importance. These supply chain networks are now facing an unprecedented challenge: the creation, production, and proliferation of tons of network equipment and billions of devices at the accelerated pace of today's innovation and subsequent consumer demand.

We work directly with these OEMs to develop scalable, customized RSCM(Reverse Supply Chain Management) solutions and give services, which include country-wide pick-up, collection, tracking of electronic assets, reverse logistics management, electronics asset recovery, data security, refurbishment, e-waste recycling, and disposal, to assure their supply chain is economically and commercially sustaining.
